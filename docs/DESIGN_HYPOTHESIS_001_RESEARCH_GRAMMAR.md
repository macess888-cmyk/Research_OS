# Hypothesis

Research may be more fundamentally represented as a semantic grammar than as a fixed sequential workflow.

The Runtime Kernel may therefore be better understood as a composition of:

- persistent entities
- research activities
- semantic relationships

rather than as a universal lifecycle pipeline.

---

# Candidate Grammar

## Ontology

Persistent research entities:

- Intent / Question
- Claim / Hypothesis
- Evidence

These are candidate durable research objects.

---

## Activities

Research transformations:

- observe
- measure
- experiment
- simulate
- analyze
- validate
- publish

These may be activities rather than first-class persistent objects.

---

## Relationships

Semantic predicates:

- questions
- supports
- refutes
- extends
- implements
- derived_from
- cites
- validates
- produces

Relationships connect research objects and define graph topology.
