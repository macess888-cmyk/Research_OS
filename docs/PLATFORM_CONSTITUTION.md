# Research OS Platform Constitution

Version 1.0

---

## Purpose

Research OS exists to improve the inspectability of research.

It does not exist to replace human judgment.

---

## Principle 1

Observation precedes interpretation.

---

## Principle 2

Signal is not authority.

---

## Principle 3

Relationships are recorded before conclusions are formed.

---

## Principle 4

Topology precedes geometry.

Geometry precedes architecture.

Architecture precedes implementation.

---

## Principle 5

Unknowns remain visible.

The platform never hides uncertainty simply to produce confidence.

UNKNOWN → HOLD

---

## Principle 6

Every capability must improve inspectability.

If a feature makes the platform harder to understand, it should be redesigned.

---

## Principle 7

Every subsystem owns one responsibility.

Coordination belongs to the Kernel.

---

## Principle 8

Events are facts.

Interpretation belongs elsewhere.

---

## Principle 9

Logging records execution.

Activity records history.

Neither replaces evidence.

---

## Principle 10

The platform remains explainable.

Every result should be traceable to observable structure.

---

## Builder's Oath

Build slowly.

Validate continuously.

Respect boundaries.

Let geometry have a say.

Leave unknowns visible.

Improve inspectability.

One layer at a time.
